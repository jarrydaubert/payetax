## UK Tax Rates for 2025-2026

### Income Tax Rates

The UK income tax rates for 2025-2026 are:

- **Personal Allowance**: £12,570 (tax-free)
- **Basic Rate**: 20% on earnings £12,571 to £50,270
- **Higher Rate**: 40% on earnings £50,271 to £125,140  
- **Additional Rate**: 45% on earnings above £125,140

### National Insurance Rates 2025-2026

- **Class 1 (Employees)**: 12% on earnings £12,570 to £50,270, then 2%
- **Class 2 (Self-employed)**: £3.45 per week if profits over £6,515
- **Class 4 (Self-employed)**: 9% on profits £12,570 to £50,270, then 2%
