# Content Philosophy

Purpose: every piece of content should be useful, accurate, and clear.

## Principles

- **Real value:** teach something concrete or remove confusion.
- **Actionable:** a reader should know what to do next.
- **Plain language:** avoid jargon unless it is defined.
- **Trust-first:** cite sources when needed and avoid exaggeration.
- **Consistent voice:** calm, helpful, and practical.

## Guardrails

- Do not hardcode tax rates or thresholds in copy.
- Avoid sensational framing around taxes.
- Be honest about calculator limitations.
