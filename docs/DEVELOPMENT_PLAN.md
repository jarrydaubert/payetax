### ❌ **Explicitly Excluded Features**
- **No Multi-language Support** - UK only, English only
- **No Self-Employment Calculations** - PAYE employees only  
- **No Company Car Benefits** - Too complex for v1
- **No International Tax** - UK PAYE focus only
- **No Contractor/IR35** - Outside scope
- **No Corporation Tax** - Not relevant to PAYE
