# READ THIS FIRST

> Build what removes blockers to acquisition, activation, retention, or monetization. Everything else is noise.

---

## Operating Principles

- **Distribution first:** Shipping is only half the work. Use real distribution to learn what matters.
- **Pick one avatar:** A focused product beats a broad one. Clarity beats coverage.
- **Painkiller over vitamin:** Build features that solve expensive or urgent problems.
- **Single source of truth:** Critical constants and business rules live in one place. Never duplicate them.
- **Backlog is the only live list:** If it’s work to be done, it lives in the backlog.

---

## What “Done” Means

A change is only “done” when:
- It matches the spec or rationale in docs.
- It is covered by a test that would catch a real regression.
- It ships without introducing new unknowns.

---

## Focus Discipline

If you can’t explain who a feature is for and what pain it removes, it’s not ready to build.

---

## Reminder

Comfortable work is not always high‑value work. Prioritize what moves the business forward.
