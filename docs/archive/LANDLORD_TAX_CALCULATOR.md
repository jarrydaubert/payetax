# Landlord Tax Calculator

## Opportunity

Target: accidental landlords who need simple guidance and clear calculations.

Why now: Making Tax Digital is expanding and HMRC is pushing digital record‑keeping for property income.

## UK Landlord Tax Rules (Conceptual)

- Rental income is added to other income and taxed at the marginal rate.
- Allowable expenses reduce taxable rental profit.
- Mortgage interest relief is a tax credit, not a deduction.
- Rent‑a‑Room scheme offers an alternative for a single room in a main home.
- Replacement of domestic items relief applies to like‑for‑like replacements.

## Calculator Features

- Rental income calculator with expenses and mortgage interest inputs.
- Tax liability estimator that shows overall impact on total income.
- Rent‑a‑Room checker to compare approaches.
