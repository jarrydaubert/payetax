**Status:** Not Started

## Concept

Accountancy firms embed branded calculator on their website. Leads flow to them, not us.

## What They Get

- Fully branded calculator (their logo, colors)
- Lead capture form → their CRM
- Client qualification analytics
- Remove "Powered by PayeTax" badge

- Embed page (`/embed`) with iframe support
