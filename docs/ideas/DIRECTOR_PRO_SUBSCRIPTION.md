# Director Pro Subscription (B2C)

**Status:** Not Started  
**Priority:** LATER (if demand)  
**Effort:** Medium

---

## Concept

Subscription for directors who want compliance paperwork automation.

## What They Get

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Calculator | Full director calculator (already free) |
| Dividend Vouchers | Auto-generated PDF vouchers |
| Board Minutes | Template minutes for dividend declarations |
| Payment Schedule | Recommended monthly draw amounts |
| Deadline Reminders | Email reminders for tax dates |

## Pricing

| Plan | Price | Notes |
|------|-------|-------|
| Monthly | £9/mo | Cancel anytime |
| Annual | £79/yr | 2 months free |

## Target Audience

- Directors without accountants
- Directors wanting to reduce accountant fees
- New directors learning the process

## Why B2C Not B2B

Originally planned as B2B for accountants, but:
- Accountants already have this in practice software (IRIS, TaxCalc)
- Directors without accountants = underserved market
- Lower price point = higher volume potential
