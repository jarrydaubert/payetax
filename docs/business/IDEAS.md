# Future Ideas

Purpose: capture exploratory ideas without committing to roadmap or scope. Active work belongs in `docs/BACKLOG.md`.

## Embeddable Calculator Widget

A lightweight, embeddable calculator for third‑party sites (HR, recruiters, finance blogs). Focus on ease of embed, clear attribution, and a low‑friction experience.

Key considerations:
- Avoid cannibalizing the main experience
- Ensure branding and referral back to core tools
- Validate demand before building

## Lead Magnet Newsletter

A simple opt‑in path for a downloadable checklist or guide that pairs with ongoing updates.

Key considerations:
- Keep content evergreen and accurate
- Use the calculator as the primary CTA
- Ensure consent and unsubscribe flow are clear

## Marriage Allowance Toggle

Add an optional toggle for Marriage Allowance transfers and eligibility checks.

Key considerations:
- Eligibility logic is strict
- Regional tax differences must be respected
- Clear user guidance to avoid incorrect claims

## PDF Export with Workings

Provide a downloadable export of calculation results and workings for record‑keeping.

Key considerations:
- Keep outputs accurate and easy to read
- Include clear disclaimer about informational intent
- Avoid hardcoding rates or thresholds in templates
